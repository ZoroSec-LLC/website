Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Have questions about cybersecurity? We’ve got answers—here’s everything you need to know about how we help protect your business.

What is a cybersecurity audit, and why do I need one?

A cybersecurity audit is a thorough evaluation of your business’s security posture to identify vulnerabilities and ensure compliance. We analyze your network, systems, and security policies to detect weaknesses before hackers can exploit them. Whether you’re looking to meet industry regulations or simply strengthen your defenses, an audit is the first step toward better cybersecurity.

How does your ongoing security monitoring work?

Our monitoring service provides real-time alerts for suspicious activity, helping prevent breaches before they happen. We use advanced tools to track unauthorized access attempts, malware infections, and other security threats. If we detect something unusual, our team acts quickly to mitigate risks and keep your business safe.

What happens if my business is hacked?

If your business experiences a cyberattack, we provide rapid incident response to contain the threat and minimize damage. Our experts will investigate the breach, remove malicious software, restore your systems, and strengthen your security to prevent future attacks. We also provide post-incident reports to help you understand what happened and how to avoid similar issues in the future.

Do I need cybersecurity if I already have antivirus software?

Antivirus software is just one layer of protection—true cybersecurity requires a multi-layered approach. While antivirus helps detect and remove malware, it doesn’t protect against phishing attacks, insider threats, or misconfigured security settings. Our services provide a comprehensive security strategy, covering all aspects of your business’s digital protection.

How do you ensure compliance with industry regulations?

We help businesses meet security standards like GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS by providing tailored compliance support. Our compliance audits identify gaps in your current security policies and processes, ensuring that you meet legal requirements. We also offer training and documentation to help your team stay compliant over time.

What makes your cybersecurity services different?

We offer personalized, expert-driven cybersecurity solutions designed specifically for small and growing businesses. Unlike generic security providers, we take a hands-on approach—providing clear, actionable recommendations, easy-to-understand reports, and ongoing support to ensure your business stays protected against evolving threats.
